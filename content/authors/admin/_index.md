Dr. Chungwon Woo is an Associate Professor in the Department of Accounting at Changwon National University. His research interests span information security disclosure, cybersecurity investment efficiency, ESG, innovation performance, and technology management. He has published 35 peer-reviewed articles in leading journals including Technology Analysis & Strategic Management, Journal of Cleaner Production, and Business Strategy and the Environment.

Before joining Changwon National University, he served as an Assistant Professor at Kyungnam University and as a Research Fellow at the Science and Technology Policy Institute (STEPI), where he managed R&D planning initiatives.
